Franny

Race: Changeling, Class: Bard

Originally destined to inherit the Ballad Weapons Trading Company, Franziska is a changeling tasked with replacing the company’s original heir. Unaware of her initial failure to kidnap the rightful daughter, she only learned of it years later, when the campaign began. By then, her resolve had shifted—Franziska no longer wished to deliver the child and sought instead to protect her, driven by guilt and fear of the child’s potential wrath. When the time came for the party—Franziska, Kelne, Aldous, Zofhime, and the child—to return home through a portal to the Prime Material Plane, her fey superiors intervened. Manipulating the portal, they forcibly took the child and Kelne into the fey realm instead, leaving Franziska with unanswered questions, a heavy conscience, and the weight of decisions she had little power to change. Even now, the consequences of that fateful moment continue to shape her journey.

Zofhime

Race: Aasimar, Class: Artificer/Warlock

Zofhime, a natural prodigy in potion-making, grew up in a humble southern Aurian village, where he became a vital resource for crafting remedies and tonics. His talent followed him into his adventuring party, where he often took the lead—though not always successfully. Over time, he transformed from relying heavily on his patron’s guidance to becoming a self-reliant individual, earning his patron’s respect along the way. During a shared dream conjured by Endelyn Moongrave, the Dame of Unhappy Endings, Zofhime witnessed the tragic death of Torin, a figure from his past. While the memory lingers, shaping his resolve, the full story of Zofhime’s growth remains intertwined with the secrets he keeps.

Aldous Caesar

Race: Half-Elf, Class: Bard/Sorcerer

Aldous Caesar, a consummate conman, has spent most of his life evading just about everything: the law, responsibility, the touch of a woman. His chaotic and unpredictable nature has often worked to his advantage, especially when it comes to lining his pockets. But could there be a reason behind the madness? Aldous guards his past like a fortress, steering clear of any conversation that might reveal it. The only glimpse into his history surfaced during a tormenting dream—a surreal experience conjured by Endelyn Moongrave, the Dame of Unhappy Endings. The party, unaware they were trapped in a dreamscape, encountered Dizzy—a woman from Aldous’s past whose sudden appearance was attributed to fey magic. In a haunting twist, Dizzy met her demise within the dream, casting a shadow of grief and mystery over her connection to Aldous. What secrets lie hidden in Aldous's peculiar past? Only time will tell how his strange history shapes the man behind the mask.

Quinn

Race: Human, Class: Fighter/Ranger

Quinn, a newcomer to the party, joined in the aftermath of the chaotic and bewildering loss of Kelne and the child during the group’s return to the Prime Material Plane from the Feywild. Wielding a perilous weapon known as 'Experiment 42,' she quickly became a focal point of the party's attention, particularly after they glimpsed a vision foretelling a future of catastrophic destruction. It was later revealed that Quinn is on the run from Harion after sabotaging a top-secret testing facility for the Ballad Company, where she had worked as an engineer. Her actions were driven by the discovery of plans for a looming war between Valios and Toleria, with Harion acting as a weapons supplier to Valios. With her family—miners employed by the Ballad Company—potentially at risk, and shadowy forces likely pursuing her, Quinn’s presence brings both uncertainty and danger to the party’s journey.
